THE SLEEP BOX

The Sleep Box is an all-in-one sleep solution in the form of a subscription box.

Insomnia affects over seventy million people worldwide. Hundreds of solutions exist, yet it remains a prominent, even growing problem around the world. That's why we created The Sleep Box. In the box, you will find a variety of products, ranging from a blood test to diagnose specific deficiences in your body, the vitamins to address those deficiencies, traditional remedies such as gummies, teas, and pills.
